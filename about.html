About Apartment

Our apartments have five cozy rooms and also accommodates 12 people, each with its own bathroom. The magnificent living room includes a cozy sofa and a fully equipped kitchen, creating a space for comfortable relaxation and pleasant pastime. From the hall you exit directly into our well-kept yard, where you can enjoy the fresh air.

Guest House Apartament City On The Palm . We are located in one of the Tbilisi districts. City On The Palm Guest House is located in Tbilisi, within walking distance of the Holy Trinity Cathedral Church. The panoramic view from the window will delight our guests, and the warm welcome will leave a pleasant impression!The staff at Apartament City On The Palm are always ready to provide information to guests. We sincerely hope that your stay at the guest house will leave only positive emotions. We will be grateful for any suggestions or wishes that will help us become better. Your comfort is paramount to us! Guest House City On The Palm is situated in Tbilisi City. All rooms boast a private bathroom. The property is 2.4 km from Rustaveli Theatre and 2.5 km from Tbilisi Opera and Ballet Theatre. The units are equipped with a bath, free toiletries and a wardrobe. Freedom Square is 2.2 km from the guest house. The nearest airport is Tbilisi International Airport, 12 km from Guest House City On The Palm.
