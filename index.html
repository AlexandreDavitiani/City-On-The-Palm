About Us

Guest House Apartament City On The Palm . We are located in one of the Tbilisi districts. City On The Palm Guest House is located in Tbilisi, within walking distance of the Holy Trinity Cathedral Church. The panoramic view from the window will delight our guests, and the warm welcome will leave a pleasant impression!The staff at Apartament City On The Palm are always ready to provide information to guests. We sincerely hope that your stay at the guest house will leave only positive emotions. We will be grateful for any suggestions or wishes that will help us become better. Your comfort is paramount to us!

Starting the day with a hearty and tasty breakfast is the key to a good mood and energy for the whole day! Our breakfast is not just food, it is a real morning celebration of taste and aroma. We offer a variety of breakfasts that will satisfy every taste and allow you to start the day right.

Khinkali is a traditional Georgian dish that has won the hearts of gourmets around the world. Try our khinkali and plunge into the atmosphere of true Georgian hospitality and culinary art. With us you will feel at home, and every khinkali will be a real discovery for you and your guests.

Khachapuri is another one of the wonders of Georgian cuisine. Come to us and plunge into the world of real Georgian cuisine. We are sure that our khachapuri and wine will leave you with the most pleasant impressions and will make you come back to us again and again!
